What is MindTrack?

MindTrack is an innovative app designed to transform the way mental health professionals manage their practice. We offer a comprehensive solution that allows you to organize appointments, record essential data, and access medical histories in a simple and secure manner. With MindTrack, you can optimize your time and enhance the quality of care you provide to your patients, all through an intuitive and easy-to-use platform!!!

Optimize Your Practice

Discover how our platform can transform your daily practice. Specifically designed for mental health professionals, this intuitive tool allows you to manage appointments, record key data, and access your patients' clinical histories quickly and securely. With MindTrack, you can optimize your time, provide more personalized and efficient care, and focus on what truly matters: the well-being of your patients.

Closer and More Personalized Care

With MindTrack, your patients will feel closer and more personalized care. The app allows you to keep detailed track of their progress, ensuring that each session is perfectly aligned with their needs and goals. By optimizing appointment management and tracking clinical histories, you can dedicate more time to what truly matters: listening, understanding, and helping your patients achieve better mental well-being.

About Us

MindTrack is a startup formed by a group of university students committed to creating an app that facilitates mental health management. Our mission is to support doctors in efficiently managing appointments, recording data, and tracking patients' clinical histories, all on an intuitive and secure platform. We aim to improve the quality of care and optimize the work of mental health professionals.
